Bypass Xiaomi Bootloader Unlock Restriction (Code 20045)

On October 20, 2024, Xiaomi began enforcing strict restrictions on the bootloader unlocking process for its devices imported from China. If a user attempts to unlock the bootloader of a Chinese Xiaomi device using a global account, they will encounter the following error:

Code 20045 – "The place where the account is registered does not match the place where the phone is sold."

What do these new restrictions mean?

Simply put, you will not be able to unlock the bootloader of a Chinese device unless you have a Xiaomi account registered in China.

Is there a way to bypass this issue?

Yes, by using a Xiaomi account registered in China.

Fortunately, there is a current glitch that allows the creation of a Xiaomi account in the China region, enabling users to bypass error 20045 and unlock the bootloader normally.

How to create a Xiaomi account in China:

  1. Clear cookies from your browser.
  2. Open the following link to create an account in the China region: Create Xiaomi Account (China)
  3. Enter a supported phone number, as some country codes may not work.
  4. Once the account is created, you can use it to attempt to unlock the bootloader on your Chinese device.

Post date: February 28, 2025 (Xiaomi may patch this glitch at any time. Therefore, if you plan to unlock the bootloader of your Chinese device, it is best to follow these steps as soon as possible before the restriction is enforced).
